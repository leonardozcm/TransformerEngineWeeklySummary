    Bug Community 2.16.0 by DmCarpe93 · merged 04-23

新增 Issues 5

  • #2918 [BUG] multi_tensor_apply: int32 overflow in TensorListMetadata::sizes causes illegal memory access
    用户 yezhengmao1 报告：multi_tensor_apply 将 per-tensor size 从 64-bit 静默截断为 32-bit（int 而非 size_t/int64），当任一输入 tensor 的 numel() > INT_MAX 时触发 illegal memory access。溢出位置在 multi_tensor_apply.cuh。
    影响：所有使用 multi_tensor_apply 且 tensor 元素数超过 ~21 亿的场景（大模型训练中并不罕见）。
    Bug Community by yezhengmao1 · 04-23
  • #2915 [BUG] TensorRT engine export fails with UNSUPPORTED_NODE errors after TE FP8 training

    Bug Community by charriry · 04-22
  • #2908 [Bug] moe_permute CUDA kernel: int32 overflow and incorrect -1 sentinel handling
    用户 jing-4369 报告 permutation.cu 中两个独立的潜在 bug：(1) int32 溢出问题和 (2) 不正确的 -1 sentinel 处理。两个 bug 在 main、v2.13、v2.9 上均可复现，自 #936（2024-08）引入后一直存在。

  • #2903 TransformerLayer implementation is overly complex and difficult to follow

    Feedback Community by Hukongtao · 04-20
  • #2902 ub_overlap_ag_fprop disabled during activation recomputation due to is_grad_enabled check
    用户 SophusDavid 报告：activation recomputation 启用时，forward pass 在 torch.no_grad() 下重新执行，导致 is_grad_enabled 为 False，无条件禁用了 Userbuffers all-gather overlap（ub_overlap_ag_fprop），使 recomputation 中的 overlap 优化完全失效。
